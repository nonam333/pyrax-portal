CRITICAL: The content should feel HUMAN-WRITTEN:
- Use varied sentence structures
- Include conversational phrases
- Add personality to descriptions
- Avoid robotic/repetitive phrasing
- Use analogies and examples where helpful
- Write like you're explaining to a smart friend
- Include occasional rhetorical questions
- Vary paragraph lengths naturally

Do NOT use obvious AI phrases like:
❌ "In today's digital age..."
❌ "It's important to note that..."
❌ "In conclusion..."
❌ "Dive deep into..."
❌ "Unlock the potential..."
❌ "Comprehensive guide to..."
❌ "Navigate the world of..."

Instead use natural, confident language:
✅ "Here's what you need to know"
✅ "Let's break this down"
✅ "Most people get this wrong"
✅ "The truth is..."
✅ "You've probably heard..."
